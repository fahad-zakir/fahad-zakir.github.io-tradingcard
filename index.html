Cat

Cats have over 20 muscles that control their ears.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Average Length: 18 in.
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Forests, deserts, urban and the homes of humans

Cats are considered one of the most popular pets in the world, they are lots of fun to play with and are very affecionate. Owning a cat can reduce the risk of stroke and heart attact by a third.
